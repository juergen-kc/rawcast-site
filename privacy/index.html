Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 8, 2026

Overview

rawcast is a weather app that respects your privacy. We collect no personal data, use no third-party analytics, and display no ads.

Weather Data

rawcast uses Apple WeatherKit to fetch weather information. Weather requests are made directly to Apple's servers through the WeatherKit framework. Apple's privacy policy governs how Apple handles these requests. rawcast does not operate any intermediary server.

Radar Data

For the radar feature, rawcast fetches publicly available NEXRAD precipitation tiles from the Iowa State University Mesonet service. These requests contain only geographic coordinates and do not include any personal information.

Location Data

rawcast requests your location only when you grant permission, and only to display weather for your current position. Location data is:

  • Processed entirely on your device
  • Never transmitted to any server operated by rawcast
  • Never shared with third parties
  • Used solely to request weather data from Apple WeatherKit

You may use rawcast without granting location access by manually adding cities.

Saved Locations

City names and coordinates you save are stored locally on your device using SwiftData. This data is not transmitted off your device except as coordinates in WeatherKit weather requests to Apple.

Analytics and Tracking

rawcast does not include any analytics frameworks, tracking pixels, or advertising SDKs. We do not collect usage data, crash reports beyond what Apple provides through standard App Store mechanisms, or any form of telemetry.

Third-Party Services

rawcast communicates with two external services:

  • Apple WeatherKit — weather data
  • Iowa State University Mesonet — radar tile imagery (US only)

No other third-party services are used.

Data Retention

All app data (saved locations, cached weather, preferences) is stored locally on your device. Deleting the app removes all associated data.

Children's Privacy

rawcast does not knowingly collect any personal information from children or any other users.

Changes to This Policy

If we update this privacy policy, the revised version will be posted at this URL with an updated date.
